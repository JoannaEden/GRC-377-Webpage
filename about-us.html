Welcome to the Garden

Eden Gates Cafe & Emporium finds inspiration from creatives in the community. We wish to create a space that inspires, comforts, and encourages all. Eden's Cafe started out as a student's summer project to make a little extra gas money in a way that would give back to the community at large. We donate to various organizations and movements monthly, each purchase you make will ultimately contribute a portion to organizations listed at the front of the shop, this list changes as people add more recommendaions of places to donate to.
